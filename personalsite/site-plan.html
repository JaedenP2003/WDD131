Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform fans about the Legend of Zelda series and different aspects of it (i.e. gear, characters, locations, etc). It will be a dynamic page that showcases various content related to the franchise. It will also display pictures and images related to the series.

Audience

My audience is those who are fans of the Legend of Zelda series and want to learn more about its various elements.

Dynamic elements

This website will utilize character cards and object lists, modals to show more detailed photos, include a map that will have various descriptions of locations upon interaction, light and dark mode depending on user preference, and filterable cards for weapons, armor, etc.
